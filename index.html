Dermatology AI Diagnostic Tool

About

The Global Burden of Disease project has revealed that skin diseases are a major health concern worldwide. Our AI-based tool aims to provide a preliminary diagnosis of dermatological conditions, contributing to improved healthcare.

Instructions

Upload an image of the dermatological manifestation for a preliminary diagnosis. Please note that this tool is not a substitute for professional medical advice, and it's essential to consult a qualified healthcare provider for accurate diagnosis and treatment.
